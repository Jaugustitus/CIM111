Website Information

For my CIM111 class I was taught how to do simple HTML, CSS, and JQuery coding. It was something that I never thought I would actually take because it does not have to do with what I want to do as my profession, but I am glad that I took it because I learned a lot of interesting things. In the slight chance that one day my boss will need a website made quickly I have the skills now to throw something together. One of the projects that we were tasked with doing was to make responsive websites about something that we are passionate about. I chose to make my websites about the New Jersey Devils and the Vegas Golden Knights.

The first part of the responive websites that I made was for the New Jersey Devils. The website is just a basic fan page for the team. On top of the page is is the Devils record from the time I made it. At the top of the page there are links to the other two cites that I made for the project.
