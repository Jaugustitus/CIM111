Magazine Information

The cover for my magazine project is of P.K. Subban, a professional hockey player, hanging onto the "G" in GQ. The main "story" in the magazine is about P.K. Subban. The article is about P.K. Subbans well know absurd outfits. The article all touches base with how much of a charitable guy that P.K. is. While playing for the Montreal Canadians P.K. Subban made a pledge to donate $10 million dollars to a hospital in Montreal. Unfortunatly for the Canadians P.K. Subban was traded to the Nashville Predators, one of the biggest blockbuster trades in a long time. The important part though is that P.K. kept his pledge to the hospital. Unlike many others who would have just gave the money and that be that, P.K. Subban regularly goes back and visits the children in the hospital.

The other article in the magazine that I chose to write about is about Chane the Rapper. He is one of my favorite artists in music now.
